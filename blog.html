About 20th Century Club of Park Ridge

Founded in 1912, our philanthropic organization is dedicated to community activities, community service, and fundraising for local charitable organizations. We also have a foundations that awards scholarships to local High School seniors. Furthermore we have monthly programs that reflect the culture and social interests of our membership.
