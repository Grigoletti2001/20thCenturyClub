Some info...

The Twentieth Century Club of Park Ridge will again award scholarships to residents of Park Ridge who are graduating from an accredited high school. Historically, 3 to 6 scholarships in the amount of $1,000 to $2,000 have been awarded each year. This year each scholarship will be awarded based on financial need, scholastic merit, and extra-curricular involvement in the school and in the community.
